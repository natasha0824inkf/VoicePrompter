Built for YouTubers, TikTokers, podcasters, teachers, and anyone who records themselves. No subscription, no server, no setup. Just paste your script and start talking.

Follows your voice

Scrolls automatically as you speak. Pauses when you stop. No hands, no foot pedal, no remote.

Works offline, forever

Install it once as an app on your phone or desktop. No internet needed after that.

Mirror mode built in

Running a physical teleprompter glass setup? One tap flips and rotates the text correctly.

34 languages, auto-detected

Paste text in any language and it figures it out. No configuration needed.

Voice commands

Say "prompter back" or "prompter forward" to jump sentences. "Prompter restart" resets to the top. Hands-free control.

Built-in video recording

Record yourself while reading your script. Choose split-screen or overlay mode. Download as MP4 when you're done.

See it in action

Install it like an app. Use it like one too.

iPhone & iPad

  1. 1 Open voiceprompter.app/app in Safari
  2. 2 Tap the Share button (square with arrow)
  3. 3 Tap "Add to Home Screen"

Android

  1. 1 Open voiceprompter.app/app in Chrome
  2. 2 Tap the three-dot menu at top right
  3. 3 Tap "Install app" or "Add to Home Screen"

Desktop

  1. 1 Open voiceprompter.app/app in Chrome or Edge
  2. 2 Click the install icon in the address bar
  3. 3 Click "Install"

No App Store required. No permissions except your microphone.
