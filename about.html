About VoicePrompter

A modern, privacy-focused voice-controlled teleprompter.

Open Teleprompter App

Key Features

  • Voice-Controlled Scrolling: Navigate your script hands-free using voice commands.
  • 100% Private: All processing happens on your device. No data leaves your browser.
  • Works Offline: Install as a PWA for complete offline access.
  • Mirror Mode: Flip text for use with teleprompter glass.

How to Use

  1. Paste Your Script: Copy and paste your text into the input field on the home page.
  2. Choose Language: Auto-detect or manually select your language.
  3. Start Teleprompter: Click the "Start Teleprompter" button.
  4. Enable Voice Control: Click the microphone button to start voice recognition.

Pro Tip: Say "prompter restart" to jump back to the beginning of your script instantly.

Features Explained

Mirror Mode

Enables horizontal flip + 180° rotation — perfect for use with teleprompter glass. The reflected text reads correctly when bounced off a mirror/glass placed in front of the camera.

Show Stop Signs

Displays visual markers (dots) at punctuation marks to help you pace your reading.

Screen Rotation

Rotates the entire app 90° clockwise — useful for iOS devices where screen orientation is locked to portrait but you want to use the teleprompter in landscape mode.
