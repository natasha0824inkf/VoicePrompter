Privacy Policy

Our Data Commitment

At VoicePrompter, we believe your data is yours. We do not collect, store, or sell your personal data. Our goal is to provide a seamless voice-activated teleprompter experience without compromising your privacy.

Website Analytics

We use highly privacy-focused, anonymized analytics on our website exclusively to understand traffic and how our tools are being used. We do not track individual users across the internet, use intrusive cookies, or collect any personal identifiable information.

Voice Processing

When you use the teleprompter on the web, your voice data is processed natively by your browser’s Speech Recognition API. VoicePrompter does not remotely transmit your audio files or transcriptions to our own backend servers.

Mac Application Privacy Policy

If you are using the VoicePrompter Mac App, please read our dedicated VoicePrompter for Mac Privacy Policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at konstantin@voiceprompter.app.
